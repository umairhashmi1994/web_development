Philosophy

The purchase of a high-quality Grand Piano or piano is a purchase for eternity.
Since almost 300 years, Grand Pianos and pianos have been made from
high-quality materials and consist of around 12,000 individual parts, some 80
percent of which are still manufactured by hand. We preserve the soul of the
instrument and breathe new life into the used Grand Pianos.In doing so,we do
not only rely on our own expertise, but also use our wealth of experience and
intuition.

Manufactures

STEINWAY & SONS GRAND PIANOS - “MADE IN GERMANY”

Hardly any other company can look back on such a rich history of success and
tradition as the company founded by Henry E. Steinway and his sons in
1853 in New York, Steinway & Sons. The instruments are manufactured in New York and
Hamburg. STEINWAY & SONS Grand Pianos and pianos have long been
standard in the music world and retain their value over time. They have all the
characteristics that are decisive worldwide.

Restoration

OUR SERVICE AROUND THE INSTRUMENT

With a great deal of experience and solid craftsmanship, Piano Müller brings the instruments back to an optical and tonal like new condition.In our workshop, our range of services includes internal cleaning of the instrument, adjustment of the mechanics, renewal of the damping, intonation of the hammer heads, as well as housing repairs: new and re-painting, refreshing of the surface up to polyester work. In addition, we also offer you the installation of an air humidifier system.
