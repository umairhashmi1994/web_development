STEINWAY & SONS GRAND PIANOS
“MADE IN GERMANY”

Hardly any other company can look back on such a rich history of success and tradition as    the company founded by Henry E. Steinway and his sons in 1853 in New York, Steinway &
Sons. The instruments are manufactured in New York and Hamburg. STEINWAY & SONS
Grand Pianos and pianos have long been standard in the music world and retain their value
over time. They have all the characteristics that are decisive worldwide.

The choice of first-class materials and, above all, the meticulous craftsmanship that still
characterize each and every Steinway & Sons, which are to this day mostly handmade, make
it a privilege to own such a jewel. The aura unique to all STEINWAY pianos emanates from
our newly restored instruments at a price that is significantly lower than that of a new
instrument. All models have a rich range of sound nuances. The midrange, treble and
basses are tonally fully balanced with the typical first-class, brilliant sound of
STEINWAY&SONS. They impress with their unmistakable versatility and are equipped with
everything that makes a Steinway so unique.

C. BECHSTEIN PIANOS & GRAND PIANOS
“MADE IN GERMANY”

In addition to many high-quality brands, we also offer used Grand Pianos
from the renowned German manufacturer C. Bechstein. The Bechstein myth draws
its inspiration from a great tradition, superior piano making and strong brands.
Since Carl Bechstein began manufacturing Pianoforte in Berlin in 1853, the
name Bechstein has stood for pianos and Grand Pianos of the highest quality.
The myth lives on - to this day Bechstein builds Grand Pianos and pianos of the
highest level.

BLÜTHNER INSTRUMENTS OF THE
FAMILY-OWNED COMPANY FROM LEIPZIG
MADE IN GERMANY

A modern manufactory with traditional craftsmanship. In addition to many
other high-quality brands, we also offer used pianos and Grand Pianos from the
renowned German manufacturer Blüthner. The Pianoforte factory Blüthner was
founded in Leipzig in 1853 by Julius Blüthner and is thereby one of the oldest
piano manufacturers in the world. The high-quality Blüthner instruments enjoy
worldwide popularity, not least because of their unique sound character.

BÖSENDORFER – THE SOUND
THAT MOVES!

Bösendorfer from Vienna is one of the oldest piano manufacturers in the world,
rich in tradition and world-famous for its unmistakably touching sound and the
outstanding quality of its instruments. The choice of first-class materials and
above all the extremely careful workmanship, which is still largely handmade to
this day, distinguish every single Bösendorfer. The product range includes
Grand Pianos in eight different sizes, from 155 to 290 cm in length, as well as
pianos. For many people, the acquisition of a Bösendorfer Grand Piano is a
life's dream.

GROTRIAN-STEINWEG PIANOS AND GRAND PIANOS FROM BRAUNSCHWEIG
"MADE IN GERMANY".

In addition to many high-quality brands, we also offer used pianos and Grand
Pianos from the renowned German manufacturer "Grotrian-Steinweg". The
Grotrian-Steinweg pianoforte factory has been family-owned for six
generations. One thing has never changed in all these years: the love of music.
It was and is the driving force - sometimes even for unusual steps. In 1830,
Friedrich Grotrian left Germany and went to Moscow as a music dealer. The
city flourished and attracted many artists such as Clara Schumann, Franz Liszt
and Arthur Rubinstein. Friedrich Grotrian knew many artists very well and had a
feeling for what was important to them in the sound of an instrument.
Therefore, he began to build pianos. Pianos loved by the artists.
