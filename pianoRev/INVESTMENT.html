Investment

Times are changing, markets are going up and down, but for more than a century and a half, every handmade Steinway instrument has grown in value. Owning a Steinway is one of the few ways to invest in traditional craftsmanship, beauty, quality and longevity. And into a valuable object that is proudly passed on to the next generation.

Our Grand Piano master workshop with over 40 years of experience in the restoration of exclusive Grand Pianos is a guarantee for your investment. All Grand Pianos are unique, handmade works of art and after our restoration, they are like a new instrument again. Our exclusive Grand Pianos have an above-average annual increase in value and cost only a fraction of a new, comparable instrument. This makes your Grand Piano an excellent investment for the future.

All instruments leave our house with a certificate certifying the craftsmanship and value retention.

A 50-year-old Steinway now achieves more than 9 times its original purchase price. The best time to invest in a Steinway Grand Piano or piano is always the same:

Now!
